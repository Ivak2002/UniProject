Our Journey

Built from the Ground Up

At IvakaComputers, our story doesn't begin with a flashy startup or major funding — it begins with a dusty desk, a few used PC parts, and a dream. In the early days, we were just a small team of tech lovers friends who were obsessed with building custom PCs and pushing hardware to its limits. We started in a garage, troubleshooting motherboards late into the night, learning the ins and outs of performance optimization, and delivering machines that outperformed expectations. What drove us wasn't profit, but passion: the thrill of helping someone get the best rig for their needs, whether they were a gamer, a content creator, or a business owner. As word spread, more people began to trust us—not just for our hardware knowledge, but for our honesty. We didn't upsell or hide behind technical jargon. We helped people make smart choices, guided by what was best for them—not for our bottom line. Today, IvakaComputers has grown into a trusted name in PC parts and custom builds. We've expanded our reach, partnered with top-tier manufacturers, and built a reputation on reliability, clarity, and world-class support. But no matter how much we grow, we still carry that same spirit that got us started—hands-on, customer-first, and always excited to build something amazing with you.

We Started with a Laggy PC and a Dream

You could say IvakaComputers was born out of frustration — the kind every gamer knows. That moment when your frame rate drops mid-boss fight, your ping spikes in the middle of a match, or your rig sounds like it's about to take off just running a game launcher. We were just a group of friends back then — gamers, modders, and hardware geeks who loved building PCs almost as much as playing on them. We couldn't afford top-of-the-line parts, but we could learn. We spent weekends tweaking BIOS settings, swapping GPUs, and helping friends fix their builds. Eventually, more people started asking for help. A Reddit post turned into a Discord channel. A couple of builds turned into a whole spreadsheet of orders. That's how IvakaComputers was born. We never set out to become a company — we just wanted to make gaming better. But that mission has stuck with us. Today, we help gamers across the country build powerful rigs without blowing their budget. We test every component, handpick our parts, and offer real advice — because we've been there. At IvakaComputers, we're not just a shop. We're your teammate, your tech support, your trusted hardware squad. Whether you're climbing the ranks or just want smooth gameplay, we've got your back. Game on.

Engineering Performance from Day One

When we started IvakaComputers, we weren't thinking about logos, marketing, or fancy product pages. We were thinking about airflow, latency, bottlenecks, and benchmarks. As engineers and system builders, we saw a gap in the market — too many people were overpaying for underperforming hardware. Too many “custom” builds were just slapped together, without thermal testing or component balancing. We knew we could do better. So we took a different approach. We brought engineering discipline into the world of PC building. We tested every configuration. We optimized cable routing for airflow. We verified PSU loads, memory compatibility, and BIOS stability. We didn't just sell parts — we delivered systems built to last, designed to perform. IvakaComputers was created for people who care about what's inside the case. For creators rendering 4K footage, gamers chasing max FPS, and developers who need absolute reliability, we're here with systems you can trust — built on data, not guesswork. We don't believe in fluff. We believe in numbers. And we'll help you find the build that delivers exactly what you need — no more, no less.

Born from the Community, Built for You

In the early days, we were just regular folks helping others on forums and Discord. We shared build advice, made compatibility charts, posted GPU reviews, and cheered when someone got their new setup running for the first time. It wasn't about business — it was about belonging. A place where no question was too basic, and every win was celebrated. Over time, more people came to us for advice — and we were happy to help. We started sourcing parts for friends, building rigs, offering honest feedback, and eventually, shipping out complete systems. Our reputation grew not because we advertised, but because people talked. And they trusted us to do right by them. That trust is everything to us. Today, IvakaComputers is bigger — we've got a storefront, a fulfillment team, and thousands of customers — but we're still rooted in the same values: community, kindness, and straight-up love for building awesome PCs. When you shop with us, you're not just placing an order. You're joining a community of builders, gamers, creators, and curious minds who love helping each other level up.
